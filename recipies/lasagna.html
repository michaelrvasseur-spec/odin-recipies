Lasagna

Classic Lasagna. A rich, hearty Italian-American classic with layers of savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

Servings 8

Get Cooking!

Ingredients

  • 12 piece lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 8.9 ounces Italian sausage (removed from casing)
  • 28.6oz crushed tomatoes
  • 3 tbs tomato paste
  • One yellow onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 4 tbs unsalted butter
  • 4 tbs all-purpose flour
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 14.3 oz shredded mozzarella
  • 3.6oz grated Parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • 10 pieces fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt and black pepper
  • 2 tbs olive oil

Steps

  1. Brown the meat. Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Add onion, cook 3 minutes. Add garlic, then ground beef and sausage. Break up meat and cook until browned. Drain excess fat
  2. Build meat sauce: add tomato paste and cook for 20 minutes. Add crushed tomators, oregano, season well with salt. Tear basil leaves and add. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes until thickend.
  3. Make the bechamel: In a saucepan, melt 4 tablespoons unsalted butter over medium heat. Whisk in 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour and cook for 8 minutes. Gradually pour in 3 cups whole milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens enough to coat a spoon.
  4. Cook the noodles: Boil 12 pieces lasagna noodles in a large pot of well-salted water according to package directions until just al dente. Drain and lay flat on a lightly oiled baking sheet to prevent sticking.
  5. Mix the ricotta layer: In a bowl, combine 16.1 ounces ricotta cheese, 1 pieces egg, half the 3.6 ounces grated Parmesan, a pinch of 1 salt and black pepper, and the remaining 10 pieces fresh basil leaves leaves (torn). Mix until smooth.
  6. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 190°C (375°F).
  7. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9×13 baking dish. Layer 3–4 noodles, then a layer of ricotta mixture, then meat sauce, then béchamel. Scatter some 14.3 ounces shredded mozzarella over the top. Repeat layers 2–3 more times, finishing with béchamel and the remaining 14.3 ounces shredded mozzarella and 3.6 ounces grated Parmesan.8Bake covered: Cover the dish tightly with foil (tent it slightly so it doesn't stick to the cheese) and bake for 30 minutes 30:00.

Bake uncovered: Remove the foil and bake for another 20–22m 30s 22:30 until the top is golden and bubbling.10Rest and serve: Remove from the oven and let the lasagna rest for at least 15 minutes 15:00 before cutting — this helps the layers hold together when sliced. Serve with a sprinkle of fresh basil.NotesMake ahead: The meat sauce can be made up to 3 days in advance and refrigerated. The fully assembled (unbaked) lasagna can be refrigerated overnight — just add 10–15 minutes to the covered baking time.
